RUET ONLINE PAYMENT SYSTEM

This is RUET ONLINE PAYMENT portal. Students can pay their fees online.
If you are an admin, click on ADMIN. From there you will be redirected to a page. Enter ACCOUNT No there. A DATABASE will be created. Then create a table by entering table name.



If you are a student, first click on STUDENT.Enter account no, roll no, name, date, amount of taka in a form. Then go to PAYMENT PAGE.
